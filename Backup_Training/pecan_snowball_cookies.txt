Pecan Snowball Cookies
-----

-----
1 1 cup - butter flavored crisco
---
1 1 3/4 cup - flour
---
1 2 TBSP - milk
---
1 1/2 cup - pecans chopped
---
1 3/4 cup - powdered sugar
---
1 1 cup - quick quaker oats
---
1 1/4 tsp - salt
---
1 1 1/2 tsp - vanilla
-----
Mix butter flavored crisco, powdered sugar, milk, and vanilla until creamy. Combine flour, oats, pecans, and salt. Add slowly to creamy mixture.
---
Shape teaspoonful into balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 15-18 minutes or until bottoms are golden brown. Roll in powdered sugar while still warm, cool completely on wire rack.
