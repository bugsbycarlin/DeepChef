Santa&#39;s Thumbprint Cookies
-----

-----
1 1 1/2 cup - butter flavored crisco
---
1 - egg
---
1 2 cups - flour
---
1 2 1/2 cups - quick quaker oats
---
1 1/2 tsp - salt
---
1 1 cup - sugar
---
1 1 TBSP - vanilla
---
1 1 3/4 cups - walnuts chopped
-----
Mixbutter flavored crisco and sugar until fluffy, add egg and vanilla. Combine oats, flour, and salt. Add slowly to butter mixture.
---
Roll dough into 1' balls and roll in nuts. Place on ungreased cookie sheet and use thumb to press in the middle. Bake for 12-15 minutes or until golden brown. Cool completely on wire rack. Fill the middle with jam or chocolate kisses (while warm)
