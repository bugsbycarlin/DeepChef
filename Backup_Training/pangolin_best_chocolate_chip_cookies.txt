Pangolin-Best Chocolate Chip Cookies
-----

-----
1/2 Teaspoon - Baking Soda
---
1 Cup - Brown Sugar Packed
---
3/4 Cup - Butter, Unsalted Melted
---
1 - Egg
---
1 - Egg Yolk Yolk Only
---
2 Cups - Flour Sifted
---
1/2 Teaspoon - Salt Sifted
---
2 Cups - Semi-Sweet Chocolate Chips Ghirardelli is best
---
1 Tablespoon - Vanilla Extract
---
1/2 Cup - White Sugar
-----
Preheat oven 325 Degrees
---
Sift Flour, Salt, Baking Soda in medium bowl, set aside
---
In medium bowl, cream together melted butter, white sugar, and brown sugar until well blended. Beat in vanilla, egg, and egg yolk until light and creamy. Mix in sifted ingredients until just blended. Stir in Chocolate Chips with wooden spoon by hand.
---
Drop cookie dough 1/4 cup at a time onto prepared cookie sheets. Cookies should be approximately 3 inches apart.
---
Bake for 15-17 Minutes until edges are lightly toasted. Cool on baking sheets first, then transfer to wire rack to fully cool.
