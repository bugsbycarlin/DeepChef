Chicken and Dumplings
-----

-----
2 tablespoons - Butter
---
4 - chicken breast
---
2 Cans - Cream of Chicken Soup
---
2 10 oz - Package of Biscuit Dough Pull apart into tiny lumps
---
1 - Yellow Onion diced
-----
Shred biscuits, put all ingredients together in crock pot and cook for 7-8 hours on low or 5-6 hours on high.
