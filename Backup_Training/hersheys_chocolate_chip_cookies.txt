Hershey&#39;s Chocolate Chip Cookies
-----

-----
1 1 tsp - baking soda
---
1 3/4 cup - brown sugar
---
1 1 cup - butter
---
1 handful - chocolate chips
---
2 - eggs
---
1 2 1/4 cup - flour
---
1 1/3 cup - hershey&#39;s cocoa
---
1 1/2 tsp - salt
---
1 3/4 cup - sugar
---
1 1/2 cup - walnuts chopped
-----
Mix butter, sugar, and brown sugar until fluffy. Add Eggs. Combine flour, cocoa, baking soda, and salt. Add slowly to butter mixture. Once all mixed add chocolate chips and walnuts.
---
Roll dough into balls and place on ungreased cookie sheet. Bake for 8-10 minutes and cool on wire rack.
