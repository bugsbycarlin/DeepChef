Florida Orange Cake
-----

-----
1/3 cup - canola oil
---
3 cups - confectioner&#39;s sugar
---
3 - eggs
---
0 - for frosting:
---
1 cup - orange juice
---
1 tbsp - orange marmalade
---
1/4 cup - softened butter
---
8 oz - softened cream cheese
---
1/3 cup - water
---
1 regular size - yellow cake mix
-----
In a large bowl, combine the cake mix, orange juice, eggs, water and oil; beat on low speed for 30 seconds. Beat on medium for 2 minutes.
---
Pour into a greased 13-in. x 9-in. baking pan. Bake at 350 for 30-35 minutes or until a toothpick inserted near the center comes out clean. Cool on a wire rack.
---
For frosting, in a small bowl, beat cream cheese and butter until smooth. Beat in orange marmalade and confectioners' sugar. Spread over cake. Store in the refrigerator.Yield:12-16 servings.
