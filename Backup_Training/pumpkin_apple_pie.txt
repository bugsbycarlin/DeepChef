Pumpkin Apple Pie
-----

-----
1 1/3 cup - brown sugar packed
---
1 2 TBSP - butter
---
1 3/4 cup - canned milk
---
1 1 1/2 tsp - cinnamon
---
1 1 1/2 tsp - cinnamon
---
1 1 TBSP - corn starch
---
1 - egg
---
1 3/4 cup - pumpkin puree
---
1 1 1/4 tsp - salt
---
1 1/4 tsp - salt
---
1 1/3 cup - sugar
---
1 3 cups - tart apples peeled, cored, and sliced
---
1 1/3 cup - water
-----
Roll out pie crust and place into bottom of pie pan. Make sure there are no air pockets underneath by pressing in bottom and sides.
---
In sauce pan combine brown sugar, corn starch, cinnamon, salt, water, and butter. Mix until smooth. Add sliced apples and stir until all the apples are coated with sauce. Pour on top of pie crust.
---
In a mixing bowl combine pumpkin, milk, sugar, egg, salt, and cinnamon. Mix together until smooth. Slowly pour over apple mixture.
---
Bake for 50-55 minutes or until toothpick comes out clean.
