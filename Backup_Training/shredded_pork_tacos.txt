Shredded Pork Tacos
-----

-----
1 2 lb - boneless, pork loin
---
1 1/8 cup - brown sugar
---
1 1 TBSP - chili powder
---
1 1 TBSP - cocoa powder
---
1 1/2 TBSP - cumin
---
1 14 oz - diced tomatoes
---
2 - jalapeno seeded and diced
---
1 1 cup - onion diced
---
1 1/2 TBSP - pepper
---
1 1/2 TBSP - salt
-----
In a small bowl mix together brown sugar, cocoa powder, chili powder, cumin, salt, and pepper. Rub seasonings onto pork, make sure to coat all of the pork, set aside. Pour tomatoes, onion, and jalapenoes into slow cooker. In a large pan over medium-high heat sear pork in olive oil, make sure to get all sides. Once seared place on top of tomato mixture. Cover and cook on low for 10 hrs.
---
Once done shred pork in crock pot with 2 forks.
