Potato Salade
-----

-----
1 1/2 cup - celery chopped
---
5 - eggs
---
1 1/2 cup - mayonnaise
---
8 - medium potatoes peeled and chopped
---
1 1/2 cup - onion chopped
---
1 packet - ranch dressing
---
1 to taste - salt and pepper
---
1 1/8 cup - water
-----
Peel and chop potatoes. In a sauce pan boil eggs and potatoes, until potatoes are soft. In a bowl mix together ranch, mayonnaise, celery, and onions. Once eggs are done, cool and peel, than chop up and add to bowl. If needed add water to get the consistancy you want. Add potatoes and salt and pepper to taste.
