Turkey Meatloaf
-----

-----
1 lb - 93% ground turkey
---
1/4 cup - cilantro chopped (or 3 frozen cubes)
---
1 - egg lightly beaten
---
1 tsp - garam marsala
---
1/2 cup - Oats, regular
---
1 cup - onions diced (1/2 small onion)
---
1/2 tsp - salt
---
1/3 cup - turkey broth
-----

---

---
Preheat oven to 350
---
Mix all ingredients gently and form into a loaf shape moundon sheet pan
---
Bake 45 Min.
---
