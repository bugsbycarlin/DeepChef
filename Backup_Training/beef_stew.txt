Beef Stew
-----

-----
2 - bay leaves
---
1 2 1/2 tsp - beef broth
---
3 - carrots peeled and sliced
---
1 - celery sliced
---
1 1/2 cup - flour
---
1 1 TBSP - garlic minced
---
1 2 TBSP - olive oil
---
1 - onion diced
---
1 1/2 tsp - pepper
---
4 - red skin potatoes cubed
---
1 1 TBSP - seasoning salt
---
1 2 lb - stew meat
---
1 2 cups - water
---
1 1/4 cup - worcestershire sauce
-----
In a ziplock bag add meat, flour, and salt. Shake until meat is evenly coated. Heat olive oil in skillet, add meat and brown all sides. Remove using a slotted spoon and place into slow cooker, add pepper.
---
Saute onion and garlic for 2 minute. Transfer to slow cooker. In skillet add worcestershire sauce, water, and beef broth. Make sure to scrape up any browned bits in pan, once mixed well turn off heat and set aside. In slow cooker add potatoes, carrots, celery, and bay leaves. Now pour broth from pan into slow cooker. Cover and cook on low for 8 hours.
