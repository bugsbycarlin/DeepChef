Cheesecake Dip
-----
A nice quick dip to take to parties when you don't have time to make a full cheesecake
-----
2 tablespoons - brown sugar
---
1 stick - butter softend
---
1 cup - chocolate chips mini
---
8 ounce - cream cheese softend
---
1 box - graham crackers
---
1/2 cup - powder sugar
---
1/2 teaspoon - vanilla extract
-----
Mix all the ingredients together in a bowl until well blended. Form into a ball and let sit in the fridge for at least 2 hours to set. Serve with graham crackers
