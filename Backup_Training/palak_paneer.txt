Palak Paneer
-----

-----
1 tsp - cumin seeds
---
5 cm - fresh ginger grated
---
600 g - frozen spinach
---
1 tsp - garam masala
---
2 - green chillies whole
---
2 tsp - ground coriander
---
2 tsp - lemon juice
---
1 - onion chopped
---
400 g - Paneer cubed
---
1/2 tsp - salt
---
3 tbsp - vegetable oil
---
4 tbsp - whipping cream
-----
Thaw the spinach in boiling water. Drain into a colander. Blend to a smooth paste in a blender or food processor.
---
Heat oil in a frying pain. Add cumin seeds and fry for 30 seconds. Add onion and fry until soft. Add the ginger and chilles and cook for another minute or so.
---
Add ground coriander and salt. Cook for another 30 seconds and add the spinach and water as necessary. The mixture should be loose but not watery. Bring to a boil and simmer for 3 minutes.
---
Add the paneer cubes, garam masala and cream. Stir and cook for a few minutes until the spinach is nice and creamy. Stir in the lemon juice to taste.
