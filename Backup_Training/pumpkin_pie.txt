Pumpkin Pie
-----

-----
1 1 tsp - cinnamon
---
2 - eggs
---
1 12 oz - Evaporated Milk
---
1 1/2 tsp - ground cloves
---
1 1/2 tsp - ground ginger
---
1 2 cups - pumpkin puree
---
1 1/2 tsp - salt
---
1 3/4 cup - sugar
-----
Roll out pie crust and place into pie pan. Make sure there are no air pockets by pressing in the bottom and sides.
---
Mix together eggs, sugar, pumpkin, milk, cloves, ginger, cinnamon, and salt. Mix until smooth, than pour on top of pie crust.
---
Bake at 425 F for 15 minutes, than turn down heat to 350 F and finsih baking for about 15 minutes, or until toothpick comes out clean.
