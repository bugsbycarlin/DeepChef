Shrimp Fried Rice
-----

-----
2 - eggs
---
2 cloves - garlic minced
---
1 1 cup - peas and carrots frozen
---
1 4 cups - rice cooked
---
1 3 TBSP - sesame oil
---
1 1/2 lb - shrimp cooked
---
1 1/4 cup - soy sauce
---
1 - white onion chopped
-----
Cook rice according to instructions. In a large skillet heat sesame oil on medium heat. Add onion, garlic, peas, and carrots. Stir fry until tender. Crack eggs into pan and scramble, mixing throught vegetables. Add rice, shrimp, and soy sauce into pan. Stir until mixed well and remove from heat.
