Rosemary Salt Crust Prime Rib
-----
For medium rare allow 15-17 min of cook time per pound of meat
-----
1 12-15 lb - boneless, Ribeye
---
1 1/2 cup - garlic minced
---
1 1/2 cup - kosher salt
---
1 1 TBSP - olive oil
---
3 - springs fresh rosemary removed from stem
---
5 - springs fresh thyme removed from stem
---
1 1/4 cup - tri-colored peppercorns crushed
-----
In a large skillet over high heat sear ribeye with olive oil, until nice golden color, about 2-3 minutes per side.
---
Crush peppercorns with a rolling pin. Shred leaves from thyme and rosemary springs into small bowl. Add salt, crushed peppercorns, and garlic to leaves. If no oil left on ribeye, rub some on all sides. Take salt mixture and rub onto ribeye, pat slightly to get to stick to the meat.
---
Roast for 20 min at 500 F, than reduce heat to 275 F and roast until meat thermometer reads 125 F (for rare/medium rare). Remove from oven and let rest for 15-20 minutes before slicing, remember that meat will continue to cook a little while resting.
