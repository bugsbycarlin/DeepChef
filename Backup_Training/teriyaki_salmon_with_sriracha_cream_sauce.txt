Teriyaki Salmon with Sriracha Cream Sauce
-----

-----
1 1/4 cup - brown sugar
---
1 1 1/2 TBSP - condensed milk
---
1 2 TBSP - cornstarch mixed with 1/4 c water
---
1 1/4 tsp - garlic powder
---
1 1/2 tsp - ground ginger
---
1 2 TBSP - honey
---
1 1/2 cup - mayonnaise
---
2 - salmon filets marinaded for 30 min
---
1 1/4 cup - soy sauce
---
1 2 1/2 TBSP - sriracha suace
-----
In small bowl, whisk together cornstarch and 1/4 cup water, set aside. In small sauce pan over medium heat, add soy sauce, brown sugar, ginger, garlic powder, honey, and 1 cup water. Bring to a simmer, and stir in cornstarch mixture until thickened. Let cool to room temperature. in ziplock bag or large bowl, combine teriyaki marinade and salmon filets, and marinade.
---
Lightly coat baking pan with non stick spray. Place salmon along with marinade in pan and bake until fish flakes about 20 min. Serve with sriracha cream sauce, and top with green onion and sesame seeds.
---

---
