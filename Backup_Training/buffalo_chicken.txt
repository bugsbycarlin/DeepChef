Buffalo Chicken
-----

-----
2 cups - Chicken shredded
---
8 oz - Cream Cheese
---
1/2 cup - Crumbled Bleu Cheese
---
1/2 cup - Frank&#39;s Red Hot Sauce or Buffalo Sauce
---
1/2 cup - Ranch Dressing
-----
Preheat oven to 350 F. Combine all ingredients and spoon into a baking dish. Bake 20 minutes, stirring regurlarly until heated throughout. Garnish with chopped green onions if desired.
