Reese&#39;s Donut
-----

-----
so many - chocolate sauce to drizzle on top
---
2 large - eggs beaten for egg wash
---
2 sheets - puff pastry
---
18 small - Reese&#39;s cups
-----
1) Pre-heat oven to 400 degrees Fahrenheit.2) Coat puff pastry in flour and roll out. Cut out circles, about 6 circles per pastry sheet.3) Place one circle on a parchment-lined baking sheet. Top pastry circle with 3 Reeses Peanut Butter Cup bites. Brush the edges with egg wash and top with another pastry circle. Carefully seal the corners. Continue assembling until complete.4) Brush the tops of the donuts with your egg wash. Bake for 15 minutes, or until donuts are golden and flaky.5) Remove from oven, top with chocolate sauce, serve while sill warm and enjoy!
