Peach Cobbler easy
-----
Delicious peach cobbler, so easy to make, so wonderful to eat!
-----
1 can - (undrained) sliced peaches dump in pan
---
1 stick - margerine melt
---
3 tablesoons - water melt with butter
---
1 box - yellow cake mix dump in pan
-----
Use a 9 x 13 pan
---
Pour a large can of sliced peaches into the pan. Slice smaller if desired.
---
Pour in Butter pecan/butter brickle cake mix on top.
---
Melt a stick of butter + add 3 tbs. water, then drizzle over top
---
Bake @ 350? for 45 minutes (check frequently from 35 mins. on to see if it is golden )
