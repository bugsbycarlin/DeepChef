Breakfast Sausage
-----
Makes big batch and able to separate later to smaller portions
-----
1 1 tsp - cayenne pepper
---
1 1 TBSP - corriander
---
1 1 1/2 TBSP - garlic powder
---
1 1/3 cup - marjoram
---
25 lbs - meat and fat
---
1 2 TBSP - nutmeg
---
1 1/2 cup - pepper
---
1 1/2 cup - rubbed sage
---
1 1/2 cup - salt
---
1 5 tsp - savory
---
1 2 1/2-3cups - warm water
-----
Mix all spices together, add water to help spread spices through meat evenly.
---
Mix in meat.
