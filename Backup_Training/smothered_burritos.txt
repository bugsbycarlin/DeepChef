Smothered Burritos
-----
Make burrito with whatever you want inside than smother sauce on top. Also can serve with rice as a side.
-----
1 3 TBSP - cornstarch mixed with water
---
1 5 oz - diced green chillis
---
1 5 oz - diced mushrooms
---
1 1 TBSP - garlic minced
---
1 1lb - ground beef
---
1 1 TBSP - jalapeno seeded and diced
---
1 1 tsp - oil
---
1 1/2 tsp - pepper
---
1 steak - pork or chicken diced thin
---
1 1 tsp - salt
---
1 4 1/2 cups - water
---
1 3 TBSP - water mixed with cornstarch
-----
In a sauce pan cook the pork or chicken with oil and garlic. Once browned add green chilis, mushrooms, salt, pepper, jalapenoes, and water. Let simmer for about 10 min. Mix together cornstarch and water and stir into sauce to thicken.
---
Brown ground meat, and get other taco fixings ready.
