Lean Chicken Bruschetta
-----
Chicken breast coved in a tomato and basil mixture.
-----
4 TSP - basil sliced
---
2 5 ounce - chicken breast
---
2 TSP - chili powder
---
2 TSP - garlic minced
---
2 TSP - olive oil
---
2 TSP - onion powder
---
2 whole - plum tomatos diced
-----
Season chciken with onion powder and chili powder
---
Grill chicken until done, about 4 minutes per side
---
Combine tomatos, basil and minced garlic season with salt and peper to taste.
---
Top chciken with tomato basil mixutre and server
