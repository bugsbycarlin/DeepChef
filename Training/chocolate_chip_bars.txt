Chocolate Chip Bars
-----
A easy and tasty recipe to throw together for drop by guests.
-----
1 bag - chocolate chips
---
2 - eggs
---
1/2 cup - oil
---
1 tsp - vanilla
---
2 tsp - water
---
1 box - yellow cake mix
-----
Mix all ingrediants together except vanilla and chocolate chips.
---
Once combined, add vanilla and mix.
---
Fold in chocolate chips.
---

---
Pour into a 9x13 inch greased pan
---
Cook for 20-22 minutes @ 350
