Lemon Rosemary Grilled Chicken Breast
-----
Lemon and rosemary are classic flavors for poultry when you let them sit on chicken breast over night the add a nice flavor.
-----
4 whole - chicken breast skinless
---
3 tablespoons - lemon juice
---
3 tablespoons - olive oil
---
1 tablespoon - rosemary
-----
In a small bowl dish whisk together olive oil, lemon juice and rosemary.
---
Add chicken and marinade to a zip lock bag.
---
Marinate for 45-60 minutes.
---
Preheat your grill. Remove chicken breast, season with salt and pepper.
---
Grill chicken over medium high heat until browned, then flip and cook until other side is browned
