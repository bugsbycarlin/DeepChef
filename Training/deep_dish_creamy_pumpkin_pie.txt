Deep Dish Creamy Pumpkin Pie
-----
A pumpkin pie with little sweetener added so that you can really taste the pumpkin and the spices
-----
2 teaspoons - cinnamon ground
---
1 9 inch - deep dish pie crust
---
2 whole - eggs
---
1 teaspoon - ground ginger
---
1 teaspoon - nutmeg
---
4 cups - pumpkin puree
---
1/2 teaspoon - salt
---
14 ounces - sweet condensed milk
-----
Preheat oven to 425 degrees
---
Combine pumpkin, condensed milk eggs and all the spices in a large bowl
---
Mix thoroughly with a wire whisk
---
Add pumpkin mixture to the pie crust
---
Bake in the over for 15 minutes then reduce the heat to 350 degrees and bake for another 35 to 40 minutes or until a knife inserted comes out clean
