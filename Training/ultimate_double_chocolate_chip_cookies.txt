Ultimate Double Chocolate Chip Cookies
-----

-----
1 3/4 tsp - baking soda
---
1 1 1/4 cup - brown sugar packed
---
1 3/4 cup - butter flavored crisco
---
1 - egg
---
1 1 3/4 cup - flour
---
1 2 TBSP - milk
---
1 1 tsp - salt
---
1 3/4 cup - semi-sweet chocolate chips
---
1 3/4 cup - semi-sweet mini chocolate chips
---
1 1 TBSP - vanilla extract
-----
Combine brown sugar, butter flavored crisco, milk, and vanilla in larg bowl. beat at medium speed until light and fluffy. Beat egg into creamed mixture. Combine flour, salt, and baking soda. Mix into creamed mixture until just blended. Stir in chocolate chips. Drop rounded tablespoonfuls of dough 3' apart onto ungreased cookie sheet. Bake 8-10 minutes for chewy cookies, or 11-13 minutes for crisp cookies. Cool 2 minutes on baking sheet on a cooling rack. Remove cookies to wire rack and cool completely.
