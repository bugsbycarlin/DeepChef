Fish Chowder
-----
Boil fish early in the day and set aside to cool, once cool debone and remove skin
-----
1 1 cup - carrots chopped
---
1 1/4 cup - celery chopped
---
1 - fish filet boil, debone and skin
---
1 4 cups - hot water
---
1 2 TBSP - lemon juice
---
1 1 cup - onion chopped
---
1 1/4 tsp - pepper
---
4 medium - potatoes peeled and chopped
---
1 2 tsp - salt
---
4 - slices of bacon cooked and chopped
---
1 14 oz - stewed tomatoes blended
---
1 1 TBSP - worcestershire sauce
-----
In large pot cook bacon until crispy, remove to cool. In pot with bacon grease add onion and celery and saute. Than add potatoes, water, stewed tomatoes, and carrots. Let simmer for about 10 min. Add fish that has been separated, salt, pepper,worcestershire sauce, and lemon juice. Cover and simmer for 1-1 1/2 hrs. When ready to serve, sprinkle on chopped bacon.
