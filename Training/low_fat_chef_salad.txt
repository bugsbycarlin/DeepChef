Low Fat Chef Salad
-----
This is a low fat chef salad that is high in protien
-----
2 ounces - avocado diced
---
6 ounces - fat free mozzerella shreaded
---
1/2 cup - hearts of palm chopped
---
8 ounces - lean low sodium ham chopped
---
6 tablespoons - low fat ranch dressing
---
1 whole - roma tomato chopped
---
4 cups - romaine lettuce
---
8 ounces - turkey breast chopped
-----
Mix all ingredients in a bowl and drizzle with the dressing
