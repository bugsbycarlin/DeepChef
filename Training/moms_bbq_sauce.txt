Mom&#39;s BBQ Sauce
-----

-----
1 1/2 tsp - cayenne pepper
---
1 1 TBSP - chili powder
---
1 1/2 cup - cider vinegar
---
3 cloves - garlic minced
---
1 1/2 cup - ketchup
---
1 - onion finely chopped
---
1 1/3 cup - sugar
---
1 2 TBSP - vegetable oil
---
1 1/4 cup - worcestershire sauce
-----
In sauce pan saute onion and garlic in oil, about 5 min. Add ketchup, cider vinegar,worcestershire sacue, sugar, chili powder, and cayenne pepper. Heat to mix flavors.
