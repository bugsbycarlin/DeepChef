Funeral Potatoes
-----

-----
1 teaspoon - black pepper
---
2 cups - cheddar cheese grated
---
1 can - Cream of Chicken Soup or cream of mushroom for vegetarian
---
1 &amp; 1/2 cup - crumbled corn flakes
---
2 lbs - hashbrown potatoes or 8 potatoes boiled til soft
---
1/2 cup - melted butter
---
2 teaspoons - salt
---
1 pint - sour cream
---
1/2 cup - yellow onion grated or minced very fine
-----
Preheat oven to 350 degrees. In a small bowl combine cornflakes and butter. In a separate bowl combine other ingredients. Press into a greased 9 x 13 pan. Top with cornflake mixture. Bake at 350 degrees for 1 hour to 1 hour 15 minutes
