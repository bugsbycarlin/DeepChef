Chocolate Coffee Overnight Oatmeal
-----

-----
1 tablespoon - cocoa powder
---
1 teaspoon - honey
---
1 teaspoon - instant coffee
---
1 dl - oat milk
---
1 dl - rolled oats raw
---
1 pinch - salt
-----
Mix everyting together.
---
Place in a jar or covered bowl. Keep in the refridgerator for at least 4 hours.
