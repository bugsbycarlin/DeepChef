Braised Pork Chops
-----
An easy herb rub gives sensational taste to these boneless pork chops that can be cooked on the stovetop in minutes.
-----
4 - boneless pork chops
---
1 1 TBSP - butter
---
1 1 tsp - dried rosemary crushed
---
1 3/4 cup - dry white wine
---
1 - garlic clove minced
---
1 1 TBSP - olive oil
---
1 1/8 tsp - pepper
---
1 1 tsp - rubbed sage
---
1 1/2 tsp - salt
-----
Combine the sage, rosemary, garlic, salt and pepper. Rub over both sides of pork chops. In a large nonstick skillet, brown chops on both sides in butter and oil.
---
Add wine to skillet cover and simmer for 8-10 minutes or until meat juices run clear.
---
When serving pour juice over pork chops
