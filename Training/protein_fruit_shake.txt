Protein Fruit Shake
-----
Great quick breakfast to get you going in the morning
-----
1 whole - banana
---
2 cups - frozen berries
---
1 teaspoon - honey
---
2 cups - skim milk
---
2 scoops - whey protein powder
---
2 tablespoons - yogurt plain
-----
Combine all the ingredients into a blender and blend till smooth
