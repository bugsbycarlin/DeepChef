Red Salsa
-----

-----
1 1/4 handful - cilantro
---
3 - fresno peppers cut ends off
---
1 handful - onion chopped
---
4 - roma tomatoes cut ends off
---
1 pinch - salt
---
2 - serrano peppers cut ends off
---
3 - shallots of garlic peel and cut ends off
---
1 - tomatillo peel and cut ends off
-----
Boil peppers for 5 min in sauce pan with water. Add tomatoes and tomatilloes and boil until tomatoes are soft. Dump water out and put peppers and tomatoes into blender. Blend in remaining ingredients and add salt to taste.
