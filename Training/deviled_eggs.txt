Deviled Eggs
-----
This is a family favorite that my grandma must make at every get together.
-----
6 - eggs
---
2 tsp - lemon juice
---
1/4 cup - miracle whip
---
2 tsp - mustard
---
1/8 tsp - pepper
---
1/4 tsp - salt
---
1/2 tsp - worcestershire sauce
-----
1. Boil eggs about 19 minutes. Let cool and cut in half.
---
2. Remove yolks and place into a bowl.
---
3. Mix all remaining ingredients in the bowl with the yolk. Mix well.
---
4. Carefully spoon yolk mixutre into the egg whites.
