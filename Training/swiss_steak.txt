swiss steak
-----
A fabulous recipe to turn a cheap type of meat into a very enjoyable meal
-----
1 whole - bell pepper julienned
---
2 tablespoons - black pepper
---
2 cans - cajun style tomatos
---
3 stalks - celery diced
---
2 tablespoons - garlic minced from jar
---
2 tablespoons - kosher salt
---
1 whole - red onion diced
---
1 teaspoon - red pepper flakes
---
1 pound - round steak cubed
-----
In a skillet combine all the ingredients and stir
---
Over medium to low heat cook for 30 minutes stirring frequently
---
Serve over mashed potatoes or other starch
