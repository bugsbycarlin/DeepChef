Banana Pancakes
-----
A nice easy to make and tasty grain free breakfast
-----
1 - banana mashed
---
0.5 tsp - cinnamon powder
---
2 - eggs beaten
---
1 pinch - salt
-----
Mash the bananas and combine with beaten eggs and all other ingredients except the oil.
---
Heat coconut oil in a pan and then add mixture. Cook for 2 minutes on a medium heat and then turn to cook the other side.
