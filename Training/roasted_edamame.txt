Roasted Edamame
-----

-----
1 teaspoon - black pepper
---
1/4 teaspoon - cayenne pepper
---
1 teaspoon - chili powder
---
1 tablespoon - extra virgin olive oil
---
1 teaspoon - garlic powder
---
1/2 teaspoon - kosher salt
---
1 teaspoon - onion powder
---
1 teaspoon - paprika
---
2 cups - soy beans frozen and shelled
-----
Preheat oven to 375 degrees F.In small bowl mix all the ingredients until coated well. Roast beans in a shallow baking dish, uncovered, for 12 to 15 minutes, stirring once, until soybeans begin to brown. Yields 4, 1/2 cup servings.
