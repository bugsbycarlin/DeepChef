Golden Rod
-----

-----
1 loaf - bread toasted
---
1 4 TBSP - butter
---
4 - eggs boiled
---
1 4 TBSP - flour
---
1 2 cups - milk
---
1 1/4 tsp - pepper
---
1 1/2 tsp - salt
-----
Boil eggs. In sauce pan melt butter, once melted add flour, salt and pepper. Stir consistently until smooth and bubbly. Remove from heat and add milk, place back over heat and stirconsistently until boiling, boil for 1 min. Set aside and peel eggs, separate the yoke into a bowl. Chop the egg whites and stir into sauce. Crush up yokes.
