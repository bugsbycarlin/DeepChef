Fish Sticks
-----

-----
1 1/2 tsp - salt
---
1 1 tsp - baking powder
---
1 - egg
---
1 - fish filet skin removed and cubed
---
1 2 tsp - garlic powder
---
1 1 TBSP - lemon juice
---
1 1 cup - milk
---
1 2 tsp - Mrs. Dash
---
1 1 cup - wheat flour
-----
In medium bowl mix together flour, milk, baking powder, salt, garlic powder, Mrs. Dash, egg, and lemon juice. Add fish to coat and marinade. Place in frier until brown.
