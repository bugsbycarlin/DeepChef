Adobo Chicken
-----

-----
2 - bay leaves
---
1 2 lb - boneless, skinless chicken thighs
---
1 1/4 cup - cider vinegar
---
4 - cooked rice
---
1 1 TBSP - minced garlic
---
1 1/4 cup - soy sauce
---
1 1 cup - water
-----
Place all ingredients into pot and bring to a boil on medium heat. Once boiling turn heat down to low cover and let cook for and hour or until broth soaks into chicken, turning once in awhile to let both sides cook evenly.
