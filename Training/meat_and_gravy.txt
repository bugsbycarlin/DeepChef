Meat and Gravy
-----

-----
4 - cooked rice
---
1 8 oz - cream of mushroom soup
---
1 clove - garlic minced
---
1 to taste - salt and pepper
---
1 1 lb - stew meat cubed to bite size
---
1 1/4 cup - water
---
1 2 TBSP - worcestershire sauce
-----
In medium pan with oil add meat, salt, pepper, and garlic, cook meat until almost done. Add mushroom soup, worcestershire sauce, and water (if there is none left from cooking the meat). Stir until smooth and meat is cooked.
