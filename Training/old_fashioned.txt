Old Fashioned
-----

-----
1 dash - Angostura bitters
---
2 ounces - bourbon
---
1 - maraschino cherry
---
1 slice - orange
---
1 teaspoon - sugar
---
1 ounce - water
-----
Stir the water and sugar until the sugar has dissolved. Add a dash of bitters. Add bourbon. Add teaspoon of maraschino cherry juice and stir. Add maraschino cherry. Fill glass with ice. Garnish with orange slice.
