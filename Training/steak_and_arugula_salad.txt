steak and arugula salad
-----
A salad you can eat for dinner becasue it is so filling! Tony Horton recommended.
-----
1 can - artichoke hearts drained and cut
---
6 cups - arugula
---
6 tablespoons - balasmic vinaigrette
---
1/2 pint - cherry tomatos
---
16 ounces - top sirloin steak
-----
Grill the steak to your specifications (rare is the best)
---
Cool and cut the steak into 1 inch slices
---
Toss together the arugula, tomatoes, and artichokes
---
Top with steak and drizzle the dreasing over dish and server
