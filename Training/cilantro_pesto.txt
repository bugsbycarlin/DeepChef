Cilantro Pesto
-----

-----
2 tsp - black pepper
---
2 cups - cilantro fresh
---
2 cloves - garlic minced
---
1/4 cup - olive oil
---
1/4 cup - parmesan cheese grated
---
1/4 cup - pinenuts roasted
---
2 tsp - salt
-----
Blend all the ingredients. Warm in a saucepan, stirring constantly, until it begins to simmer.
