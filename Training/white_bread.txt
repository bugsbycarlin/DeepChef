White bread
-----
For cinnimon rolls heat oven to 350 F and bake for 25-30 minutes, or until golden brown.
-----
1 - egg
---
1 8 cups - flour
---
1 1 1/2 tsp - salt
---
1 1 TBSP - sugar
---
1 1/2 cup - sugar
---
1 1/2 cup - vegetable oil
---
1 1/2 cup - warm water
---
1 2 cups - warm water
---
1 1 TBSP - yeast
-----
In a large mixing bowl combine yeast, sugar, and water. Set aside to let yeast rise. In medium bowl add water, vegetable oil, salt, and sugar microwave for 1 minute. Beat in egg and add to yeast mixture. Slowly mix in flour, keep adding flour until dough is not too sticky.
---
Once dough is mixed, place in large greased bowl and lightly cover. Let dough rise above the rim of the bowl.
---
Divide dough in half and place in greased bread pans. Let rise to top of bread pan before placing in oven.
---
Cinnimon rolls:
---
Roll out dough so its about 1/2" thick. Spread butter over dough, sprinkle brown sugar, and cinnimon, raisins, and nuts. Roll up and cut into 1" pieces. Place onto greased cookie sheet and let rise some before placing in oven.
