Green Salsa
-----

-----
1 1/4 handfull - cilantro
---
2 - jalepeno peppers cut ends off
---
3 - roma tomatoes cut ends off
---
1 dash - salt
---
3 - serrano pepper cut ends off
---
3 - shallots of garlic peel and cut ends off
---
2 - tomatilloes peel and cut ends off
-----
Peel tomatilloes and garlic, also cut off ends of peppers and tomatoes. Mix all in blender until smooth, add salt to taste.
